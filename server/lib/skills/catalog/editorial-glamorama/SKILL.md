## What this skill adds

### Typography (Premium Mix)
- **Heading**: "Inter Tight" (Sans-Serif, Uppercase, Tight tracking)
- **Body**: "Nanum Myeongjo" (Serif, Elegant, High readability)
- **Nav**: "Inter Tight" (Uppercase, Wide tracking)

### Color Palette (Organic High-End)
- **Primary**: Deep Charcoal (`#272d25`)
- **Accent**: Warm Sand (`#e2c7b7`)
- **Text**: Deep Grey (`#4a4544`)
- **Background**: Soft White (`#fdfdfd`)

### Layout
- **Full-Bleed Hero**: High-impact imagery with centered display text.
- **Asymmetric Grid**: Moving away from rigid 3-column cards to a dynamic editorial layout.
- **Minimal Navigation**: Sticky-glass or minimal top-bar to prioritize content imagery.

### Notes
This skill transforms standard "database" cards into high-fashion editorial blocks. 
Best paired with high-quality photography from Unsplash.
